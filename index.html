How To Play

 Simply press the letter on your keyboard that you think is in the mystery word. A correct guess will show up in the mystery word. A wrong guess will begin "the hanging." Get all the correct letters and you will recieve a reward. If you run out of guesses, you'll only get "The Hanged Man".
 Good Luck!
